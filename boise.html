Boise, Idaho

The Capital and Cultural Heart of Idaho

Demographics

  • Population: ~240,000
  • Median Age: 36.2 years
  • Median Household Income: $68,000
  • Average Salary: $55,000/year

Cost of Living

Boise offers a relatively moderate cost of living compared to other western U.S. cities. Housing has grown but remains more affordable than Seattle or Denver.

  • Average Rent (1BR): $1,200/month
  • Home Price (Median): $430,000
  • Utilities: ~$150/month

Weather

Boise has four distinct seasons with hot summers and cold winters:

  • Summer Avg: 90°F (32°C)
  • Winter Avg: 30°F (-1°C)
  • Annual Snowfall: ~20 inches
  • Annual Sunshine: ~210 days

Explore the Beautiful City of Boise

Boise offers a vibrant mix of culture, outdoor activities, and great food. From historic neighborhoods to bustling downtown, there’s always something exciting to discover in Idaho’s capital city.

Float the Boise River

During the summer months, floating down the Boise River is a popular and relaxing way to enjoy the outdoors. Bring your friends and cool off on this scenic urban waterway.

Hike Wonderful Table Rock

Table Rock offers stunning views of Boise and the surrounding valleys. The hike is rewarding and perfect for locals and visitors who want a quick escape to nature right outside the city.
